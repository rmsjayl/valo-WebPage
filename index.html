Valorant

It's a multiplayer first-person shooter (FPS) in which one team assaults while the other defends in a 5v5 match. Search and Destroy, the basic game mode, is quite similar to CS:GO.

The assaulting side's purpose is to plant and explode a bomb known as a spike , while the defensive team seeks to avoid it.

Regardless matter whether the spike is planted or not, the other squad will win if a squad is wiped out before any other victory condition is satisfied.
